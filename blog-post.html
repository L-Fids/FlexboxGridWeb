Basic Sourdough Recipe

  • Logan Fidler
  • May 30, 2020

This recipe is the workhorse in your bread stable. If you learn to make this well, branching off into other varieties of bread will be easy and natural.

Flour, water, salt and a levain (AKA a leavening agent) are all you need to get this recipe going. The following proportion of ingredients will render you two smaller sized loaves. Perfect for an individual or small family.

White Flour: 604 grams

Whole Wheat Flour: 276 grams

Water: 684 grams

Fine Sea Salt: 22 grams

Levain: 216 grams
